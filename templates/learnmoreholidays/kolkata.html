Discover Kolkata

Experience the cultural capital of India - where tradition and intellect thrive

Kolkata Holiday Package Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights
  • Ideal for: Culture Enthusiasts, Food Lovers, History Buffs
  • Best Time: October to March

Top Attractions

Victoria Memorial

Magnificent marble building dedicated to Queen Victoria, surrounded by lush gardens.

Howrah Bridge

Iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River, a symbol of Kolkata.

College Street

World's largest book market and hub of intellectual discourse.

Dakshineswar Temple

Beautiful Kali temple on the banks of Hooghly River.

Cultural Experiences

  • Street food at Park Street
  • Traditional Bengali thali experience
  • Famous Bengali sweets tasting
  • Coffee at College Street's Indian Coffee House
