Top Attractions

Gateway of India

Mumbai's most iconic landmark, built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary.

Marine Drive

The 'Queen's Necklace' - a 3.6-kilometer-long boulevard offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea.

Siddhivinayak Temple

One of the most popular temples in Mumbai, dedicated to Lord Ganesha.

Colaba Causeway

Famous street shopping destination with countless stores and food joints.
