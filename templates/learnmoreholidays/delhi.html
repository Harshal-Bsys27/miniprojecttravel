Top Attractions

Red Fort

UNESCO World Heritage site and symbol of India's independence. Experience the Sound and Light show in the evening.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture at its finest.

India Gate

War memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers, beautiful at night with surrounding gardens.

Humayun's Tomb

UNESCO site that inspired the Taj Mahal's architecture, surrounded by beautiful gardens.
